Phoenix

Phoenix is a web-based, personal portal for tiny Phoenix Community and dedicated to the projects and experiences which have been done by students majoring at ADA University. The portal puts together online project resources you would need most, containing straightforward and centralized access to acquaintanceship with Members, brief information about #Project Experiences. How this web-based page would help you? You will, - get more information about ADA university - see the photos/videos and report from first project of Phoenix Group (what Hour of Code is and more) - make contact with members directly, so that they would be grateful to support you in any topic related to project experiences.

Why named as Phoenix? Interested?

The Phoenix is a mythological bird known throughout all cultures and all ages. When it dies, the bird bursts into flames and is reborn from its ashes, making it immortal. Each life is said to be between 500 and 1000 years. Crimson and gold accent most depictions of this majestic creature, although they may also have other colors such as blue or purple. With the attractiveness of a peacock and the size of an eagle, this bird symbolizes beauty and strength. The Phoenix's cry is considered to be a beautiful sound, much like a beautiful song. Through its beauty and unusual death, the Phoenix has come to be a symbol of immortality, renewal, and rebirth. Although no one knows for sure how the myth of the Phoenix began, the origins trace as far back as biblical times and within every continent where people live. Some believe its legend began from mysterious birds that people indeed came across. Most likely, no one will ever know.

Since the phoenix symbolizes birth, death, and rebirth as well as eternity; We as a whole Phoenix Community wants our page to symbolize and convey persistence of learn, apply and review as well as experience.
